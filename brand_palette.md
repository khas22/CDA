## Logo Concept
Monogram “CDA” inspired by the VW lockup:
- A bold circular mark.
- **C** forms the outer ring.
- **D** is a stylized inner arc, balanced to the right.
- **A** is a geometric letterform nested centrally.
Use one-color navy on light backgrounds; white knockout on dark backgrounds.
